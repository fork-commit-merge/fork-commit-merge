\section{Fractions}
Typesetting fractions in LaTeX:
\[
\frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad + bc}{bd}
\]
In this equation, $a$, $b$, $c$, and $d$ represent numerical values.

\section{Integrals}
Typesetting integrals in LaTeX:
\[
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx
\]
Here, $a$ and $b$ represent the lower and upper limits of integration, respectively.

\section{Matrices}
Typesetting a 2x2 matrix in LaTeX:
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
\]
In this matrix, $a$, $b$, $c$, and $d$ are the matrix entries. Entry $a$ is in row 1, column 1; entry $b$ is in row 1, column 2; entry $c$ is in row 2, column 1; and entry $d$ is in row 2, column 2.
