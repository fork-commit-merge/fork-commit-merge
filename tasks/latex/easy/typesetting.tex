\section*{Fractions}
\[
\frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad + bc}{bd}
\]
This equation shows how to add two fractions with different denominators.

\section*{Integrals}
\[
\int_a^b f(x) \, dx
\]
Where $a$ is the lower limit and $b$ is the upper limit of integration. This represents the definite integral of $f(x)$ from $a$ to $b$.

\section*{Matrices}
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
\]
This is a $2 \times 2$ matrix where $a$, $b$, $c$, and $d$ are the matrix elements.
