% Fractions section
\section*{Fractions}
Typesetting fractions is useful for displaying mathematical expressions clearly.
Consider the example:
\[
\frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad + bc}{bd}
\]
Where $a$, $b$, $c$, and $d$ are integers, and $b, d \neq 0$.

% Integrals section
\section*{Integrals}
Integrals are fundamental in calculus. Here is a basic integral:
\[
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx
\]
Where $a$ is the lower limit and $b$ is the upper limit of integration.

% Matrices section
\section*{Matrices}
Matrices are rectangular arrays of numbers. Here is a 2x2 matrix:
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
\]
Where $a$, $b$, $c$, and $d$ are the entries of the matrix.
