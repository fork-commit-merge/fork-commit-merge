\section*{Fractions}
A basic fraction example: $\frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad + bc}{bd}$

\section*{Integrals}
The definite integral is defined as: $\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx$, where $a$ and $b$ are the lower and upper bounds of integration.

\section*{Matrices}
A simple 2x2 matrix is represented as:
\[
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
\]
Each entry represents an element in the matrix.
